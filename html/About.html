About:

This Website is for selling my Artworks and Displaying them for others to see.
aswell as giving Daily Updates and Blogging.

Where I Started:

I'm very fond of fantasy as I grew up playing mostly rpg games because those are the only good/fun games available on mobile phones, games with high customization, some of the games that gave me drive to create artwork was Iruna(phone rpg game), another is Toram(sister to Iruna).
These games were the place where I first started to sell Art Commissions, selling artworks for In-game Currency.

Where I Currently Am:

Recently I fell into the rabbit hole that is D&D, the table top role playing game(ttrpg) that greatly feeds my starving Imagination.
I really enjoy it, played as both a Game Master and Player, both are fun but I think I prefer being the player, because I don't know anything, and not knowing anything is the most fun state of a game, being a noob at first is trully where I get the most joy.

I've had my drawing tablet since May 1 2018, she's held on but months ago the pen I was using, the pen that came with the tablet stopped working, I may have dropped it a ton before it stopped working, but hey 5 years, it lasted pretty long, so yes, I am working to hopefully get an upgrade for my Old but still strong and handy equipment.

What my current Activities are:

I've been rather busy with college activities, so artworks aren't at the top of my priority list, but it is high in my desires. I'm itching to draw and learn more of the elements of what makes a good Image, I have also been trying to learn a bit of animation.
Hopefully I'll be able to return to creating more artworks soon.
